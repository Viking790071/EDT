Designed to monitor tasks.

To open this report, use the Tasks expiring on date command in the Task reports list in Business processes section.

Users can easily monitor their tasks through this report. The report display a summary of tasks assigned to a user that expire on the date specified in the Deadline field. Breakdown by Author, Importance, Task, Deadline is supported.

Filters by Importance and Author of tasks are also supported.

By default, tasks expiring tomorrow morning or earlier are displayed in the report.
