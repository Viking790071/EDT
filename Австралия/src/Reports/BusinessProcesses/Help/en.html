Designed to monitor business processes.

It is only issued for completed business processes.

Opened by the Business processes list command in the Task reports list of the Business processes and tasks section.

Report options

  • Business processes. Allows you to conduct a comparative analysis of the frequency of occurrence of various types of business processes in the application. Business processes are grouped by kinds, and description, author, the flag of start and end, the start and end date of the business process are displayed in the report strings. The report is generated for the Period.
    • You can set the filter by Business process kind.
  • Statistics on types of business processes. Allows you to view the report as a graph. The report is generated for the Period.
    • You can select statistics for one of the Business process kinds.
