Use this report to monitor tasks. By default, all overdue tasks performed during the current month are displayed in the report.

To open this report, use the Tasks completed with delay command in the Task reports list in Business processes and tasks section.

The tasks in the report are grouped by assignee. The report displays task names, importance, planned completion dates, actual completion dates, and number of days overdue for each task. The report is generated for a period. Filters by Period and Author are supported.
