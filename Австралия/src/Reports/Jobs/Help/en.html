Designed to monitor the Job business process.

Opened by the Job list for a period command from the Task reports list of the Business processes and tasks section.

Report options

  • Job list for a period - designed to monitor the procedure of the Job business processes. The report strings show the business process description, the status of its completion (Yes or No), the author and the start date, as well as how many days the business process lasted and how many cycles occurred during the process. You can display all, completed, or only incomplete jobs. The report is generated for the Period.
    • Filter by Period and by Author is available.
  • Statistics of job execution-check cycles. The report (in the form of a chart) in a visual form presents a comparison of the number of jobs execution-check cycles by Authors. Set Period in the report.
    • You can filter only by the completed jobs and by Author.
  • Average duration of job execution - designed to control job execution due dates. The report (in the form of a chart) presents in a visual form the longest business processes for a certain Period for analysis.
    • You can filter jobs for Period and by Author.
  • Statistics of job execution-check cycles. The report (in the form of a chart) in a visual form presents how many jobs are performed and how many are completed. The report is generated for the certain Period.
    • You can filter only by the completed jobs and by Author.
