1C: Drive

1C:Drive is a complete solution for managing key processes in small to mid-size businesses. . Bringing transparency to operations, the solution allows real-time tracking of financial KPIs, monitoring performance of individual business units with smooth information flow between Sales, Purchases, Production, Customers Service and other. With 1C:Drive, managers are empowered with data for taking critical decisions and driving their business to new opportunities.

  • Sales and Purchases

Close deals faster with the ability to copy existing customer or vendor invoices or create new ones with just one click. Stay updated on the sales order and shipment status. Manage sales plans fulfillment, individual or consolidated.

 

  • CRM

Speed-up order-to-cash process, keeping you customer base with all the order history organized. Track pipeline. Keep your communication under control with all the agreements, discounts and obligations by your hand.

 

  • Inventory & Warehouse

Control stock levels and prevent product outages. During the order entry process, having instant access to inventory allows you to give customers accurate shipping estimates on-the-fly.  

 

  • Human Resources

Calculate payroll, manage employee workload and efficiency.

 

  • Retail Sales & Cash Operations  

Cash sales are instantly posted to your system and no additional entries or processes are needed to monitor your right-now cash position. Direct integration with cash registers

 

  • Assembly & Light Manufacturing

Track the status of production orders. Estimate materials required for production. Calculate cost of goods.

 

  • Financial Reporting

Maintain control over your company’s finances. Alleviate the stress of suddenly having to deal with lack of funds. Get fast, customizable access to financial information and reporting tools for making critical business decisions.
