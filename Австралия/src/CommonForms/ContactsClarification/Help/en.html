It is designed to save addresses of incoming email senders to the application contacts.

You can also map email addresses of an email with contacts and their current contact information in the application.

To open it, click Specify contacts link in the incoming email. If contacts were mapped automatically, this link is not displayed. In this case, you can use the Specify contacts command of the More menu.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Address in email. This column contains all email addresses that an incoming email has.
  • Save in contacts:
    • Contact. Select a contact for mapping from the application lists. If a contact has not been mapped yet, the Select contact window will open. Using it, you can pick the corresponding application list.
    • Contact email kind is picked from the contact automatically.

Mapping contacts

  • If the address and the contact were previously mapped, it will be displayed in the list.
  • If the address and the contact were not previously mapped, or if it is necessary to map the address and anther contact, or to write the address in another field of contact information, in the Contact field you can select a contact to write the new email address. In the Contact email kind field you can specify the field to which the contact email address will be written.
  • Click the Write and close button to rewrite addresses of the specified contacts to the specified email kind.
  • After mapping, a contact description will be added to email address (its presentation).
  • If a contact email address is specified in the application, then, when you receive an email from such a contact, the contact is mapped to the email address automatically.
