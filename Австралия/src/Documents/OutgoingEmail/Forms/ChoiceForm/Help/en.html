This list is used to select an Outgoing email document.

To open the list, choose the Outgoing email type (for example, when filling the Subject field in an interaction document) and click .

The list includes the following columns:

  • Created on. The document creation date.
  • Number. Assigned automatically.
  • Subject. The brief description of the email topic.
  • Subject. The subject of the interaction document. For example, "Customer claim". Subject is adopted from an application document or interaction document. If the interaction subject is not specified, it is automatically copied from the current document, meaning that interaction chain starts with this interaction document.
  • Recipients. Contact persons specified as recipients for the email.

Selecting documents

  • Select a document, then click Select.
  • , or double-click it.

Entering an Outgoing email document

  • Click Create. Fill in the required fields.

Forwarding an outgoing email

  • Select an outgoing email.
  • Click Forward. For forwarded email, recipients are not filled in automatically. This creates an outgoing email that contains the text and attachments of the selected outgoing email.

Printing an email

  • Click Print.
  • In the Print window, specify printer settings, such as number of copies, paper size, and more.
