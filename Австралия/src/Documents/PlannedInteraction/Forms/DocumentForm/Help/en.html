Fill in the following required fields:
  • Subject of the interaction, for example, customer claims. Subject is adopted from an application document or interaction document. If the interaction subject is not defined, the current document serves as a subject. It means that such document begins the interactions thread.
    • To fill in this field, click Select, choose data type, and select a document from the list.
  • Subject. Brief description of what the scheduled interaction is about.
  • Person responsible. Select a person responsible for the interaction from the users list. By default, for the most of interaction documents, the current user is automatically specified as a person responsible.
  • Reviewed. Indicates that the interaction is reviewed and does not require further attention. Reviewed documents are excluded from selection. We recommend that you use the Reviewed check box upon sharing interaction documents with your team.
  • Review after. Set the date and time when you want to review the interaction. Select one of the following values from the list: In 15 min, In 30 min, In 1 hour, In 3 hours, or Tomorrow. To set a custom date and time, click .
  • Importance. Indicates whether the interaction is highly important or insignificant. Possible values are Low, Normal, and High.
  • Details. Detailed description of what the scheduled interaction is about. See the associated tab.
  • Comment. An arbitrary text comment. See the associated tab.
  • Created. Date and time the document is created. The field is filled automatically with the date and time when the document was written. The field is not editable.
  • Author. A user who created the document. The field is populated automatically. The field is not editable.

You can set the application settings to show or hide the Reviewed and Review after fields. You can change the settings in Administration - Organizer.

Members

To record contacts participating in an event, use the Members list. Select the Members tab. Add information on meeting participants to the tabular section of the document. Select Add. In the list, you can see participant names and contact details. The list must contain at least one participant.

  • The list includes the following columns:
    • Contact presentation. A full contact name.
    • Contact details. Participant's phone, address, email, and so on.
    • Contact. Participant picklist.
  • If the participant is already registered in the application, their information is specified in the Contact field. ClickSelect to find a contact in the Select contact dialog box. The Contact presentation and Contact details fields are filled in automatically.
  • If you are not going to store information on the meeting participant, specify Contact presentation and fill in the Contact details field. In this case, you can see the information message Not all contacts selected.
  • If it is a new participant, you can register the contact after saving the document by clicking Create contact. Select a list to save the contact. Fill in the required fields.

Creating interaction documents based on a scheduled interactions

    • Schedule meeting
    • Send SMS
    • Send email

Viewing interactions created based on a scheduled interaction

  • Click Interactions on the navigation panel to go to the list of interactions created based on this scheduled interaction.
