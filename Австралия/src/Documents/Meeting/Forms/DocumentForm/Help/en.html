Fill in the following required fields:

  • Started on. The date and time the meeting started.
  • Ended on.The date and time the meeting ended.
  • Subject. Describes the main point of the meeting. Select Select and specify Data type. Then, select an interaction document or application document from the list. If you didn't specify the interaction subject, the Subject field is populated with the current value, and the document starts a new interaction thread. When you select Interaction, the new document inherits the subject and main fields from the parent document. This creates an interaction thread.
  • Location. Address where the meeting took place.
  • Topic. Brief description of the meeting.
  • Person responsible. Person from the participant list who is in charge for the meeting. By default, for the most of interaction documents, the active user is selected as the person responsible.
  • Reviewed. Indicates that the interaction has been considered and requires no further attention. Reviewed documents are excluded from selection. It is recommended to use Reviewed check box when sharing interaction documents with your team.
  • Review later. Set a value if you want to postpone the meeting review. The following default values are available: In 15 min, In 30 min, In 1 hour, In 3 hours, or Tomorrow. Select to set a custom date.
  • Importance. Label the interaction depending on the level of attention it requires. The available labels are: Low, Normal, High. Each level of importance has its own icon.
  • Description. Describes the meeting details. The field is required. See the associated tab.
  • Comment. An arbitrary text comment. See the associated tab.
  • Created. The date and time the document was created. The field is filled automatically with the date and time when the document was written. The field is not editable.
  • Author. A user who created the document. The field is populated automatically. The field is not editable.

You can set the application settings to show or hide the Reviewed and Review after fields. You can change the settings in Administration - Organizer.

Participants

    In the Participants tab, you can mention the contacts who took part in the meeting. Select the Participants tab. Add information on meeting participants to the tabular section of the document. Select Add. Specify participant name and contact details. The list must contain at least one participant.

    • The list includes the following columns:
      • Contact presentation. A full contact name.
      • Contact details. Participant's phone, address, email, and so on.
      • Contact. Participant picklist.
    • If the participant is already registered in the application, their information is specified in the Contact field. In the Select contact window, find the contact and click Select. Contact presentation and Contact details are populated automatically.
    • If you do not want to save a participant in the application, you can fill in only Contact presentation and Contact details. In this case, you can see the information message Not all contacts selected.
    • To register a contact in the application, save the document, then select Create contact. Select a list to save the contact. Fill in the required fields.

    Generating interactions from meetings

    • Click Interaction and choose one of the following:
      • Schedule interaction
      • Schedule meeting
      • Send SMS
      • Send email
      • Make call

    Viewing interactions generated from meetings

    • To view the list of interactions generated from a document, on the navigation panel, click Interactions.
