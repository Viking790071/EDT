How to view interactions created based on a phone call?

Fill in the following required fields:
  • Subject of the phone call, for example, customer claims. Subject is adopted from an application document or interaction document. If the interaction subject is not defined, the value of the current document is automatically specified as a subject. It means that such document begins the interactions thread. When you click Interactions from the document, the new document inherits the interaction subject and main fields from the parent document.
  • To fill in this field, click Select, choose data type, and select a document from the list.
  • Select the check box to indicate that the phone call is Incoming. If the check box is cleared, the phone call is Outgoing.
  • Importance. Indicates whether the interaction is highly important or insignificant. Possible values are Low, Normal (default value), and High. Importance is displayed in the interactions list by special icons.
  • Person responsible. Select a person responsible for the phone call from the users list. By default, for the most of interaction documents, the current user is selected as a person responsible.
  • Review after. Fill in the field if the call phone review is deferred until a particular date. Select one of the following values from the list: In 15 min, In 30 min, In 1 hour, In 3 hours, or Tomorrow. To set a custom date and time, click .
  • Reviewed. Indicates that the phone call is reviewed and does not require further attention. Reviewed documents are excluded from selection. We recommend that you use the Reviewed check box upon sharing interaction documents with your team.
  • Subject. Short description of what the phone call is about.
  • Details. Detailed description of the phone call. Specify which actions are necessary within the interaction. The field is required.
  • Comment. An arbitrary text comment, additional information on the phone call.
  • Created. Date and time the document is created. The field is filled automatically with the date and time when the document was written. The field is not editable.
  • Author. A user who created the document. The field is populated automatically. The field is not editable.

You can set the application settings to show or hide the Reviewed and Review after fields. You can change the settings in Administration - Organizer.

Caller
  • If the caller is already registered in the application, their information is specified in the Contact field. ClickSelect to find a contact in the Select contact list. After you select a contact, the Caller and Contact details fields are filled in automatically.
  • If you are not going to store information on the caller (for example, if you have a phone call with a new counterparty and have not decided yet whether you will work with them in the future), then fill in the Contact details field and specify a text presentation in the Caller field. Near the Contact field, the application displays the warning message: Contact is not selected.
  • To register a contact directly from the phone call, click More actions - Create contact. Select a list, click OK, and then specify contact data and fill in the rest of the document.

Creating interaction documents based on a phone call
  • Click Interaction and choose one of the following:
    • Schedule interaction
    • Schedule meeting
    • Send SMS
    • Send email
    • Make call

Viewing interactions created based on a phone call

  • Click Interactions on the navigation panel to go to the list of interactions created based on this phone call.
