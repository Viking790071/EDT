Use this window to manage the Job business processes.

This business process consists of two steps: execution and check. The supervisor decides whether or not to return the task for revision.

The tasks for execution and check, generated by the application on the Job business process, is addressed to performer not only explicitly, using personal addressing, but also using role addressing.

Opened by the Job command in the Business processes and tasks section.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Importance. Takes Normal (default), Low, High values. Use this field to specify that execution of a business process can be postponed or, to the contrary, should be accelerated.
  • Number. Number is automatically entered by the application, one of the icons is placed next to the number, indicating the state of the Job business process.
  • Date. Data and time of creating the Job business process.
  • Job. Full description.
  • Due date. Date and time by which Job is to be completed.
  • Completed. Execution date and time.

Filters

  • Application automatically selects jobs by the Author field for the current user. You can select jobs by any Author. To do that, select them from the list of users.
  • To select jobs by the Performer field, you can also use the list of users. In the case of role addressing, the list of role performers is available.

Viewing all jobs

  • Select the matching check boxes to Show completed and stopped jobs. Check boxes are cleared by default.

Entering new job

  • Select Create and fill in the required fields.

Business process record management

  • The button can Stop a business process that has been started.
  • Selecting Continue, you can resume the execution of a stopped business process at any time.
