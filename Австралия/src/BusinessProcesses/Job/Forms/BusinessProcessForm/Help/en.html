Предназначено для постановки и проверки выполнения произвольных задач.

You can start the form from the application document using the Create on basis command (you can also start it from the business process; with the appropriate settings this job will become a subordinate business process). Также можно начать новый бизнес-процесс Задание по команде Создать в списке Задания.

Info on job

  • Main task. Filled automatically if the business process is subordinate. The reference in this field allows you to view data about the main business process.
  • Job. Write a job title, which will be shown in the lists of business processes. This field is required.
  • Importance. Takes Normal (by default), Low, High values. To select a business process from the list, or to specify that the execution of this business process might be deterred, select the matching value of this field.
  • Performer. Select a performer from the application users list or from the performers list. The fields are required.
  • Due date. Specify data and time by which to completed the job.
  • Subject. An application document is automatically entered, on the basis of which the job is created.
  • In the text box below you can explain in more detail the essence of the job. Unavailable for subordinate business processes.
  • Select the Check execution check box (enabled by default) in order to:
    • Assign supervisor from the users list.
    • Enter Due date. Date and time by which to complete the execution check. If the due time is not filled, the check is not carried out.
  • Author. Application automatically enters the current user. Author can be assigned from the Users list.
  • Started. The date of job creation. Filled automatically. The field cannot be edited.

Business Process Start

  • After the job is filled, click Start and close to save Job, start the business process, and return to the program.
  • To start a business process, you can also use the Start command. В этом случае программа сохранит задание, начнет бизнес-процесс, но задание останется открытым.
  • The application automatically generates the tasks contained in the Job business process:
    • выполнение задания;
      • After completing this task, depending on the settings, the job is either completed, or the task of execution check is generated.
  • To write a draft of the job without starting it, click Write. Such a job can be refilled and started later, at a more suitable time, from the jobs list or by opening the job.

Deferred start of business process

  • You can start the business process not immediately, but to defer its start for a certain time. For example, you can schedule its start for tomorrow or in a week.
  • To set a deferred start of the job, after filling in all the required fields, click .
    • In the window, select the Deferred job start check box.
    • Fill the Date and Time fields - specify when to start a business process. The bottom of the window, you can see in how many dates and hours the job will be started.
    • Click Finish.
  • After that, a message with a reference is displayed in the business process card where the date and time of the deferred start is indicated.
  • To start deferred business processes at the right time, configure the Deferred business processes start scheduled job.
  • After the job is started, the deferred start button becomes inactive.

Business process record management

  • To Stop or Continue the business process, use the matching buttons (one of the buttons is active). In some lists a stopped business process is marked by the icon.

View flowcharts

  • Click Open the business process flowchart to view the business process in a visual form.
  • You can also use the More - Flowchart command.
  • The Flowchart presents internal logic and lifecycle of a business process as a schematic image, a sequence of route points.

View tasks

  • To view tasks of the business process, execute the Tasks command in the navigation panel. If the job is not started, the list will be empty.
