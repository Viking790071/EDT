This list is used to display the latest information about the map of users and roles, and addressing objects. This information is used by the business process logic to assign tasks to specific performers.

Depending on the specifics of business processes, tasks can be addressed to a performer or to a group of performers both personally (personal addressing) and, for some business processes, using business process roles (role addressing). In case of role addressing, assignment is not issued to the performer, but to a certain role of the business process (for example, Room supervisor).

Peculiarities of interaction with external users

  • External users can be performers of some business processes (for example, partners or contact persons of partners).
  • As a rule, external users cannot use the names of specific employees of a company, thus, they can only specify the performer roles assigned to them.
  • To do that, you need to configure a list of such roles specifying that they are designed for certain categories of external users.

The map of performers to roles and addressing object is set before starting work with business processes and tasks, and it is used by the application for delivering instructions to specific performers.
