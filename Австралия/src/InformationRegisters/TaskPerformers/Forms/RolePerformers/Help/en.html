This list is used to display the latest information about map of users and roles, and addressing objects. This information is used by the business process logic to assign tasks to specific users.

Depending on the specifics of business processes, tasks can be addressed to a performer or to a group of performers both personally (personal addressing) and, for some business processes, using business process roles (role addressing). In case of role addressing, assignment is not issued to the performer, but to a certain role of the business process (for example, Room supervisor).
