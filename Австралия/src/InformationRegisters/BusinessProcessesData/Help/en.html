This list is used to display business processes created by the current user.

Opened by the My business process in the Business processes and tasks section.

The list includes the following columns:

  • Number. Assigned automatically. Next to the number, the application automatically puts one of the icons indicating the status of the business process.
  • Description. Full description of a business process.
  • Date. Data and time of creating a business process.
  • Completed. Application automatically specifies data and time of business process execution.

Inclusion in the list of completed business processes

  • By checking the matching box at the bottom of the window, you can also display completed business processes in the list (unchecked by default).

Business process operation

  • Select a business process (for example, Job) in the list and select Change.

View the business process flowchart

  • Select Route map to view the procedure of business process execution visually.
